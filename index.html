Yoga With Denis

Yoga is a great way to rejuvenate both your body and mind. During the pandemic, I did not have access to gyms or weights. So I decided to focus on becoming more flexible and endurant with cardio and yoga.

I primarily follow youtube tutorials since I am a beginner to yoga. I try to mix up the videos I follow to try to expose myself to new stretches.

You get out of yoga the effort you put in. You are in control in yoga and have full autonomy over how intense you want the stretches to be.

The advantages of yoga are manifold. One is that it makes your body less prone to injuries when lifting heavy weight. It is also a form of meditation and a way to remove oneself and even overcome the stresses of daily life.

Some of the stretches I am working on now are the boat pose and crane pose. One of my favorites is the revolved side angle pose.
